About PureOKRs.com

What are Objectives and Key Results (OKRs)?

It is a goal setting methodology for operationalizing a company's strategy. Objectives are main goals, while key results are what you need to accomplish to reach the main goals. OKRs are organized in a tree structure starting with high level OKRs which are broken down into smaller and smaller OKRs as the tree is traversed. The methodology is used by all kinds of companies - from small tech startups to industrial giants.

Why PureOKRs?

There are lots of OKR tools out there. However, most of them are unnecessary complex and make it difficult to maintain the elegant simplicity of the OKR methodology. The purpose of PureOKRs is to provide a tool that helps you keep track of your Objectives and Key Results. No more, no less.

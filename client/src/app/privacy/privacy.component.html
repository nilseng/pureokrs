Privacy at PureOKRs.com
Cookies
  • PureOKRs is using Google Analytics to measure traffic on the website. Google Analytics is making this possible by the use of cookies.
  • In order to provide a secure and user friendly solution, PureOKRs is storing a token for authentication purposes in your browser. The token is deleted if the user logs out or when it expires after a set interval.
Processing of personal data
PureOKRs is storing names to make it possible to show you who the other users from your company are, and to make it possible to assign OKRs to individuals. In addition, email addresses are stored in order to send invitation emails to new users and to send emails in case someone needs to reset their password. Both names and emails are deleted permanently when users are deleted from PureOKRs.

The data is stored in document databases provided by MongoDB Atlas. The servers on which the databases are run, are provided by AWS and are located in Europe.
